Services

Four engagement shapes. One way of working.

Each engagement starts with a written plan: what we're delivering, what success looks like, what it costs, and when it ends. No open scopes.

01

Implementation

Stand up Salesforce the right way — from greenfield org to first close — without disrupting the business in flight.

What you get

  • Discovery, success criteria, and a written engagement plan
  • Org design: objects, fields, record types, profiles, permission sets
  • Data migration and de-duplication
  • Integrations with your stack (email, finance, marketing, support)
  • User training and a documented go-live plan
Best for
Teams adopting Salesforce for the first time, or replacing a starter CRM.
Typical timeline
6–12 weeks
Pricing
Fixed fee, scoped after discovery
02

Optimization

Untangle an org that's grown messy. Audit, simplify, automate, and ship the workflow your team actually needs.

What you get

  • Org health audit (technical debt, security, automation, reports)
  • Prioritized backlog with effort/impact scoring
  • Process automation in Flow, replacing legacy workflow rules and process builders
  • Reports & dashboards refresh
  • Documentation of the resulting system
Best for
Teams with an aging org that no longer matches how the business runs.
Typical timeline
4–10 weeks
Pricing
Fixed fee for audit, then T&M or fixed for execution
03

Admin-as-a-Service

An on-call senior admin without the headcount. Predictable monthly support that scales with the business.

What you get

  • Backlog triage, prioritization, and biweekly releases
  • User support, training, and onboarding
  • Proactive monitoring of org health and security
  • Quarterly roadmap review with business stakeholders
  • Documentation kept current as the org evolves
Best for
Teams without a full-time Salesforce admin, or supplementing one.
Typical timeline
Monthly retainer, no minimum term
Pricing
Tiered retainers from part-time to embedded
04

Training & Enablement

Equip your team to own Salesforce day-to-day. Role-based training, runbooks, and admin handoff.

What you get

  • Role-based end-user training (sales, service, ops)
  • Custom runbooks and short video walkthroughs
  • Admin enablement and handoff
  • Office hours during the first 60 days post-launch
Best for
Teams that need adoption to stick, or are bringing admin in-house.
Typical timeline
2–6 weeks
Pricing
Per-cohort or per-role
